Description

The McLaren 765LT is an extreme, track-focused supercar designed to deliver raw performance and driving purity. As part of McLaren’s “Longtail” (LT) lineup, it emphasizes lightweight construction, enhanced aerodynamics, and sharper handling compared to standard models. At its core is a 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine producing around 755 horsepower, paired with a 7-speed dual-clutch transmission.
