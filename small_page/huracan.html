Description

The Lamborghini Huracán is a modern supercar known for its sharp design, naturally aspirated V10 engine, and thrilling driving experience. It sits in Lamborghini’s lineup as a more “everyday usable” supercar compared to the Aventador, while still delivering extreme performance. Power comes from a 5.2-liter naturally aspirated V10 engine that produces around 610 to 640 horsepower depending on the variant. This allows the Huracán to accelerate from 0 to 100 km/h in roughly 2.9 to 3.4 seconds, depending on the model and drivetrain (RWD or AWD).
